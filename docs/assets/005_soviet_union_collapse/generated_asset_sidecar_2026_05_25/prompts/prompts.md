# Event 005 Generated Flag Prompts

## PRA Flag Family

Use case: historical-scene
Asset type: HOI4 fictional alternate-history flag source sheet for Event 005 Soviet Collapse.
Primary request: Create five distinct flat rectangular flag designs for the Pale Railway Authority: base, communist variant, democratic variant, fascist variant, and neutrality variant. These are fictional route/ideology flags, not real historical flags.
Composition: one clean horizontal row of five separate 82:52 aspect-ratio rectangular flag panels, equal size, with generous spacing between panels. No labels and no readable text.
Visual direction: period-plausible 1930s-1940s railway emergency authority heraldry; strong central motifs such as rail tracks, signal lamps, crossed switch keys, station clock silhouette, telegraph wires, depot seal, snow rail line, and austere civil-rail administration symbols. Each variant must have a genuinely different composition, emblem, and palette, not a simple recolor.
Style: crisp flat flag art with subtle aged cloth/printed texture, high contrast, readable at 82x52, 41x26, and 10x7. Avoid modern logos, modern typography, flags copied from existing countries, small unreadable detail, watermarks, labels, UI artifacts, fake folds, waving fabric, flagpoles, perspective, shadows, and generated text.

## MFR Ideology Flag Family

Use case: historical-scene
Asset type: HOI4 fictional alternate-history ideology flag source sheet for Event 005 Soviet Collapse.
Primary request: Create four distinct flat rectangular ideology flag designs for the Munitions Factory Republic: communist, democratic, fascist, and neutrality variants. These are fictional factory-state ideology flags; preserve the existing no-suffix base flag concept by making these variants related to industrial munitions authority without copying or recoloring a single base.
Composition: one clean horizontal row of four separate 82:52 aspect-ratio rectangular flag panels, equal size, with generous spacing between panels. No labels and no readable text.
Visual direction: 1930s-1940s emergency industrial republic heraldry; strong motifs such as factory rooflines, gear-and-shell emblems, foundry cranes, safety chevrons, inspection stamps without text, smokestacks, rivets, arsenal keys, workers' council seal, and ammunition depot symbols. Each ideology variant must have a genuinely different emblem structure and palette, not a simple recolor.
Style: crisp flat flag art with subtle aged cloth/printed texture, high contrast, readable at 82x52, 41x26, and 10x7. Avoid modern logos, readable lettering, watermarks, labels, UI artifacts, fake folds, waving fabric, flagpoles, perspective, shadows, and flags copied from existing countries.
