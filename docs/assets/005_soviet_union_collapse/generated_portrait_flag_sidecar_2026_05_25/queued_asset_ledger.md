# Queued Asset Ledger

This sidecar keeps the live dirty `gfx/flags/`, `gfx/leaders/`, `interface/`, gameplay, localisation, history, country, focus, event, and spreadsheet files untouched. It identifies the generated-art scope that should be reviewed or regenerated in an isolated pass before any parent-agent promotion.

## Scope Rules

- Existing-country no-suffix base flags are out of scope and must remain vanilla or repository-approved unless a spec explicitly creates a cosmetic route tag.
- Historical or historically attested flags and symbols should be routed to `chaosx_asset_source_researcher`, not generated as fictional art.
- Fictional or alternate-history custom tags and ideology/route variants may be generated by `chaosx_generated_event_art`.
- Each promoted flag needs normal, medium, and small TGAs, plus a contact sheet proving orientation at all sizes.
