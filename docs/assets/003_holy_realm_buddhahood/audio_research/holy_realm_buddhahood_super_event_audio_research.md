# Holy Realm Buddhahood Super-Event Audio Research

Bounded scope note:

- This pass followed the audio-only super-event brief.
- I did not audit existing repo `music/` reuse or uniqueness because the parent prompt did not grant that repo-scan surface.
- I rejected unclear, commercial, YouTube-only, and vague "royalty free" sources.
- I favored Wikimedia Commons file pages with explicit license statements because they provide stable redistribution terms and direct original-file downloads.

## Risks and blockers

- No hard blocker remains for `The Awakened One`, `Powers of the Awakened`, or non-terminal `Final Silence`.
- The terminal `Shika no Tōne` candidate is usable, but its confidence is lower than the CC0 and CC BY chant recordings because the public-domain chain relies on the Commons rights rationale for an early Japanese recording. If the parent wants only the clearest modern explicit-license chain, replace the terminal variant with another CC0 or CC BY chant rather than this one.
- I did not perform a repo-wide collision check against existing super-event tracks or sound ids in this pass.
