## Super-event A, Fury becomes a major

Working key:

- `super_event_fury_major`

Trigger:

- the first Fury country crosses the implemented major threshold, becomes a vanilla major, or reaches a documented custom threshold of controlled states, factories, and divisions
- fire once per campaign

Role:

- escalation from strange minor war to campaign-shaping power

Title direction:

- short and specific
- possible working titles: `No Longer Minor`, `The Small State Ascends`, `A New Major Marches`

Description direction:

- explain that a country once dismissed as a border problem has become a major military actor
- keep uncertainty about whether this can be contained

Quote direction:

- real quote about small states, ambition, war, expansion, or the danger of underestimating weak actors
- verify wording and attribution
- do not invent a quote

Button text direction:

- short, cold, or ironic
- no generic `OK`

Image direction:

- generated super-event image, 457x328
- small-state command room transformed into a larger war office
- maps, officers, border arrows, stark period atmosphere
- no readable text

Audio direction:

- music, not sound effect
- public domain or clearly licensed
- 1 to 2 minutes preferred
- document composition and recording rights separately

## Super-event B, War Without Borders

Working key:

- `super_event_fury_world_end`

Trigger:

- Fury world-end branch activates according to the spec

Role:

- terminal world-end scenario where Fury spreads to other continents

Title direction:

- `War Without Borders` unless research finds a stronger short title

Description direction:

- present it as a collapse of geographic containment
- explain that new Fury states are igniting beyond the original continent
- make terminal state clear without overexplaining script logic

Quote direction:

- real quote about war spreading, borders failing, ambition, fate, or political contagion
- source and attribution must be verified

Button text direction:

- short and grim

Image direction:

- generated super-event image, 457x328
- several continents or map rooms marked by military arrows
- no modern UI, no readable generated text

Audio direction:

- darker than the major-threshold super-event
- still musical
- licensing must be clear

Deliverables:

- `docs/super_events/007_fury_super_event_research.md`
- quote candidates and selected quote with confidence
- button text candidates and source notes if references are used
- audio candidates, license notes, final recommendation, and editing notes
- image handoff notes for the asset worker
- uncertainty notes
