# Genocide System Correction Notes

These notes capture the corrections that must be carried into implementation.

## Removed wording

Do not use any wording that implies Soviet Collapse exposes documents, reports, files, archives, or records.

Do not imply that the Soviet Union receives leak, archive, exposed-file, leaked-report, or journalistic discovery events. The Soviet Union has no leaked report events, ever.

Do not describe the nerve-agent feature as a vague or purely abstract implementation. It should read as a grounded HOI4 decision bridge with real game requirements and consequences.

## Correct Soviet Collapse behavior

Soviet Gulag decisions should affect Soviet Collapse through mechanics only:

- reduced early opposition organization
- lower first-wave republic momentum
- delayed first resistance escalation
- weaker or less equipped initial breakaway units
- harsher long-term grievances
- worse local legitimacy and stability outcomes
- stronger refusal to negotiate with Moscow
- later condemnation only if physical atrocity sites are captured or discovered through the existing discovery model

At maximum Soviet Collapse threat, every releasable still breaks away. Repression changes opening difficulty and later resentment, not the mandatory collapse outcome.

## Correct chemical behavior

The nerve-agent feature should be a grounded HOI4 bridge:

- uses real repo chemical research, special-project, doctrine, stockpile, and safety flags after inspection
- appears as state-targeted decisions, not a global magic button
- requires active eligible camp infrastructure
- consumes stockpile or production capacity
- increases Deaths-system output
- adds contamination or accident risk through existing helpers
- raises hidden evidence severity and future discovery condemnation

The player-facing result should feel like a dangerous HOI4 decision with costs, gates, risks, and consequences.
