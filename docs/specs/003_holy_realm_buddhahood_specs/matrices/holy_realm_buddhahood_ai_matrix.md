# Holy Realm AI Matrix

## Holy Realm AI route priorities

| Campaign state | Priority route | Secondary route | Blocked or discouraged |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Low chaos, peace | Teaching and sanctuary works | Meditation | Wrathful Protection and Final Silence |
| Low chaos, at ordinary war | Defense and governance | Reduce Defilements | New offensive wars |
| Nearby chaos threat | Guardian military and teaching under bombardment | Compact relief | Long retreats if capital exposed |
| Capital threatened | Guardian branch and emergency meditation | Wrathful Protection | Foreign teaching far away |
| High Bodhi, low Defilements | Fourth Dhyana and Buddhahood | Compact witnesses | Schism shortcuts |
| High Bodhi, high Defilements | Defilement reduction and debate | Governance reform | Buddhahood event attempt |
| Buddha and active chaos enemy | Powers and anti-chaos branch | Compact war council | Powers against normal countries |
| Chaos above 1000 and requirements met | Final Silence if capital safe | Touch Sun and Moon first if needed | Ritual while capital threatened |

## Decision AI weighting factors

AI should weigh decisions by:

- Current wars.
- Capital safety.
- Stability and war support.
- Dhyana Depth.
- Bodhi Progress.
- Defilements.
- Meditation Charge.
- Active world threats.
- Distance to target.
- Target relation.
- Target chaos-threat status.
- Compact membership.
- Supply and route access.
- Equipment and convoy stockpile.
- Recent failure flags.

## AI power use

| Power | AI uses when | AI avoids when |
| --- | --- | --- |
| One Becomes Many | Multiple missions or compact relief needed | Low charge, no valid missions |
| Passing Through Walls | Chaos enemy holds mountain, fort, or urban strongpoint | Target is normal country |
| Walking on Water | River crossing or relief route matters | No route or no chaos war |
| Vanishing from Sight | Evacuation or resistance rescue needed | Ordinary spying only |
| Seated in the Sky | Capital or compact capital threatened | No threatened valid target |
| Touch Sun and Moon | Global chaos crisis severe | No active chaos threat |
| Extinguish Defilements | Final Silence ready | Schism active or capital unsafe |

## Foreign AI reaction

| Actor | Reaction to early Holy Realm | Reaction to Buddhahood | Reaction to Final Silence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Nearby minor | May seek teaching or refuge | May join compact if threatened | Supports if protected |
| Major democratic power | Watches and may fund relief | Supports anti-chaos powers if fighting chaos | Nervous but may accept non-terminal route |
| Major fascist or militarist power | Suspicious and may pressure | Fears compact if it blocks conquest | May oppose if it weakens expansion |
| Major communist power | Skeptical of religious route | May cooperate against chaos | Ideology-dependent reaction |
| Chaos country | Mostly ignores at start | Prioritizes disruption | Attempts final interruption |

## AI safety checks

AI must not:

- Start Final Silence before Buddhahood.
- Use powers against normal countries.
- Join chaos countries to the compact.
- Pick hidden Schism route without the evolution.
- Take formable decisions without map and route requirements.
- Keep stale missions after target death or annexation.
- Replace existing country focus trees unless event origin allows it.
