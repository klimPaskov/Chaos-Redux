# Holy Realm Research Notes

This spec uses Buddhist concepts as inspiration for a fictional Chaos Redux alternate-history event. Final localisation should stay careful and sourced when using direct religious quotations.

## Source anchors used for design direction

DN 2, the Sāmaññaphala Sutta, includes a passage on psychic or supernormal powers that inspired the gameplay translations of becoming many, passing through walls, walking on water, vanishing, and moving through the air.

SN 51.20, the Iddhipādavibhaṅga Sutta, connects the four bases of psychic power with such abilities. This inspired the four-part meditation interface model using Intention, Energy, Mind, and Investigation.

Theravada jhana explanations describe jhanas as deep meditative unification and present the four jhanas as a graded concentration sequence. This inspired Dhyana Depth 0 to 4.

The spec does not select final super-event quotes. The super-event research pass must verify wording, attribution, and translation before using any quote.

## Design translation

The powers should be treated as in-game supernatural manifestations, not claims about real-world religious practice.

| Textual motif | In-game translation |
| --- | --- |
| One becomes many | More teaching slots, simultaneous relief, coordination |
| Goes through walls and mountains | Fort, mountain, and strongpoint advantage against chaos |
| Walks on water | River and relief-route power |
| Appears and vanishes | Intel, rescue, and evasion tools |
| Travels through the air | Emergency redeploy and supply relief |
| Touches sun and moon | Rare global anti-chaos morale and reveal power |
| Extinction of defilements | Final Silence route, not ordinary combat power |

## Respect and tone notes

Avoid joking button text around Nirvana or Buddhahood. Use restraint. The dramatic effect should come from silence, discipline, and impossible calm.

Avoid making the Holy Realm a caricature of pacifism. It can defend, fight chaos, and protect refugees, but its clean route should punish predatory conquest.

Avoid making powers available as ordinary magic buttons. Meditation is the gate.

## Open research tasks for implementation

- Quote candidates for `The Awakened One`.
- Quote candidates for `Powers of the Awakened` that frame powers as lesser than liberation.
- Quote candidates for `The Final Silence` related to Nirvana or cessation.
- Public domain or clearly licensed audio for each super-event.
- Sourced historical symbols if real Buddhist symbols or flags are used.
- Any real portrait if the implementation chooses a real figure instead of a fictional Bodhisattva.
