## Why this source was selected

- It matches the requested diplomatic congress room and treaty-table mood.
- It avoids flag-forward framing.
- It is period-authentic and visually legible at Chaos Redux super-event size.
- The curved table and attentive delegates communicate bloc formation and formal recognition better than crowds, map rooms, or military scenes.

## Known uncertainty

- The photograph is a real League of Nations Council meeting, not a literal congress of newly independent small states.
- Commons identifies the session as one where Ribbentrop is addressing the council. The chosen crop avoids foregrounding that identity, but the underlying historical context remains broader than Event 006's exact fictional league.
