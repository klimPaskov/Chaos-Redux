## Executive Findings

1. No current same-focus-chain duplicate `add_ideas` was found by the mechanical scan. That specific duplicate-helper spam appears cleaned up in the current worktree.
2. The reward-spam complaint still has merit because broad helper-mediated idea churn remains. Several route-defining focuses clear whole families of tension/staged ideas, and many focus rewards still read as spirit/counter maintenance instead of distinct mechanics.
