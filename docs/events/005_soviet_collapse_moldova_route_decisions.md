# Event 005 Moldova Route Decisions

Moldova uses route decisions tied to the focus paths that discuss the Prut statute, Romanian alignment, the Dniester line, and Odessa-Chisinau customs traffic.

The Romanian and Prut decisions give Moldova recognition, resilience, military help, and formal statute support. They also create patronage strain, so leaning on Romania is useful but politically risky. The Sfat decisions let Moldova hold that pressure back by writing limits into public council authority.

The Dniester decision turns the river route into a military line with bridge defenses, anti-air, and stronger depot control. The Odessa-Chisinau customs decision supports the Ukrainian border and League-observer route with rail, convoy, liaison, and infrastructure gains.
