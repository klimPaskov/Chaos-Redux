## Changes From This Audit Pass

The 2026-05-23 focus-quality follow-up replaced several repeated adjacent equipment-dump rewards with more distinct route mechanics:

- `blr_soviet_collapse_minsk_supplies_the_front` now emphasizes rail movement and command coordination instead of another infantry/support dump.
- `blr_soviet_collapse_the_league_depot_at_minsk` now builds depot control, infrastructure, and a supply node instead of repeating the previous front-supply equipment reward.
- `kaz_soviet_collapse_horse_and_truck_columns` now rewards mobile command experience and institutional consolidation instead of repeating manpower/equipment from the horse-register path.
- `kaz_soviet_collapse_rail_guard_brigades` now unlocks armored train technology where needed and keeps the rail/depot identity without another infantry-equipment grant.
- `internal_soviet_collapse_volga_crossing_militias` and `internal_soviet_collapse_bashkir_cavalry_oath` now split into crossing fortification/command and cavalry command/experience payoffs rather than adjacent medium infantry-equipment rewards.

The custom splinter endgame repair wires `BSC`, `TNC`, `ALA`, `UDC`, `SDZ`, `GAC`, `DHC`, `KHC`, `UWD`, and `NLC` ordinary endgame focuses to their existing route-specific completion helpers. These focuses no longer end as generic high-chaos identity plus legal-recognition/stability packages.

The later custom-splinter semantics pass separates route commitment from final completion for the highest-risk duplicated helper calls. `FTH_extreme_path`, `KRS_extreme_path`, `FEV_extreme_path`, `SZA_extreme_path`, and `ARD_extreme_path` now apply high-chaos commitment rewards instead of firing final endgame helpers before their capstones. `KRS_every_harbor_a_soviet` remains an intermediate naval-port consolidation focus and no longer fires the final port-council helper before `KRS_endgame`. `NLC_endgame` is now the shared polar settlement root; the actual final helper remains on the mutually exclusive follow-up endings. `soviet_collapse_complete_arctic_directorate_endgame` now sets only Arctic Directorate identity and no longer sets the Kronstadt port-council flag or Every Port a Council super-event.

The reward-quality pass reduces the most visible custom-splinter payoff loops. FTH commune logistics now trades repeated stockpile injections for command power, recognition, League preparation, stability, political power, XP, and a smaller tachanka motorization payoff. KRS port consolidation now leans into naval XP, convoy capacity, command power, coastal radar/air coverage, and fortress morale instead of repeating train, infantry, support-equipment, and manpower grants. FEV and SZA rail/supply chains now diverge: FEV receives naval/convoy and dispatch command payoffs, while SZA receives command, political, recognition, and stability payoffs around Siberian zemstvo control.
